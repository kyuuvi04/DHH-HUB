"Seedhe Maut"

Seedhe Maut, a Delhi-based hip-hop duo, embarked on a remarkable journey in the Indian music scene. Comprising Encore ABJ and Calm, their journey began with underground rap battles and local performances. With gritty lyrics and hard-hitting beats, they quickly gained recognition for their raw and authentic style. Seedhe Maut's breakout moment came with their debut album "Bayaan," which catapulted them into the spotlight of the Indian hip-hop scene. Their relentless dedication to their craft and unwavering passion for music have earned them a loyal fanbase and critical acclaim. As they continue to push boundaries and challenge norms, Seedhe Maut remains at the forefront of India's burgeoning hip-hop movement, inspiring aspiring artists and leaving an indelible mark on the industry.
