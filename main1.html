Desi Hip-Hop

Desi hip hop blends South Asian music with hip hop, originating in urban India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh.

It integrates traditional instruments like the tabla and sitar with modern beats and samples, creating a unique sound.

Beyond music, it's a movement that addresses social issues and empowers marginalized voices.

Desi hip hop is gaining global momentum, celebrating the diversity of South Asian expression.
